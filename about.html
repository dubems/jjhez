Who we are & what we do

J.J Hez is a Nigerian Company offering integrated construction solutions and related services. The company's scope covers all areas of construction,including pre and post phases. J.J Hez traces its operation back to the construction of the OTIGBA crescent road located at G.R.A Onitsha. A major infrastructure project in the megacity of Anambra state.

Years of steady growth followed this pioneer project during which J.J Hez grew to become the dynamic Nigerian company it is today. Throughout this time, J.J Hez deployed progressive construction methods to ensure that innovation and quality are priotized for the benefits of clients. The company business is built on the strategy of having vertically integrated operations which improve efficiency and timeless of project execution.

Additionally, subsidiaries support J.J Hez's ability to offer a complete range of construction related services. J.J Hez's head office is located in Onitsha, Anambra State with additional permanent locations in Lagos and Port-Hacourt. The company is also represented across the nation in structural engineering and infrastructure works.

The company aspires to operate its business in a manner that exceeds the ethical, legal, economical and public expectation of the society. Values include a pledge to ethnical conduct integrity and respect in all aspects of the company's ongoing training of employees and the establishment of collaborative partnerships with clients. J.J Heez holds itself accountable for being honest, fair and respectful in all aspect of its business and operates in a zero tolerance atmosphere for any action that could be perceived as contrary to these values.

Everything you should know about us

In 1986,J.J Hez was awarded a contract to engineer, build and construct an infrastructural project in Nigeria, the Otigba Crescent Road and Drainages in Onitsha Anambra State. This was the foundation of J.J Hez's Commitment to Nigeria.

Following this intial project the company began to diversify its portfolio an its growth started to run in parallel to the developement of Nigeria. Througout this time J.J Hez's role in the development was also evolving proven to be a company that consistently delivered reliable solutions, J.J Hez became a pivotal partner to G.R.A Onitsha Landlord's in the construction of Omu Crescent Road. Otherprojects including Buildings,establishing Road signs in Enugu during Governor Chimaroke's regime. Today,J.J Hez's construction company Limited maintains its role as an integral partner in Nigeria's construction industry.

The construction business of J.J Hez is the heart of the J.J Hez Constrution Company Limited subsidiaries contribute to this core business, making it possible to offer broad range of products and services. This structures allows J.J Hez to effectively manage and fufil construction projects,starting from the initial idea, through to design and engineering construction and maintenance. J.J Hez's value is built on the belief that the company's duty, apartfrom delivery the highest quality standard in work, is to support and advance the development of its staff, host communication and the country of Nigeria itself.
